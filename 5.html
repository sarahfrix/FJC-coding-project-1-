Frequently asked questions

  • Are you currently accepting new patients?

    • Yes!

  • What services do you offer?

    • We are a full service family and internal medicine practice, offering comprehensive healthcare and care for minor emergencies 7 days a week

  • What insurance do you accept?

    • We currently accept Medicare, most commercial insurance plans and all Tennessee care plans except Amerigroup

  • Where are your locations?

    • We have 6 different locations all located in west Tennessee, our locations page has specifics on the locations and addresses to each of our locations.

  • Can I go to any of you clinic locations?

    • Yes, all clinic locations have access to your electronic medical records and are happy to see you if you in that area and need to be seen as a patient.
